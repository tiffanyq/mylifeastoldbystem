about
"my life as told by STEM" is a run by an Engineering Physics student. she likes relating the concepts she learns in class to her life, because she finds that it:
  • humanizes STEM
  • helps her remember and understand concepts
  • gives her comfort
she hopes "my life as told by STEM" also helps you with conceptual understanding, navigating life, or both; if you're not into this kind of thing, she hopes that it at least gives you something to eyeroll at.
